Cricket

Cricket is a popular sport played between two teams of eleven players each.
It is played using a bat and a ball on a circular or oval field with a rectangular pitch at the center.

The main formats of cricket are Test matches, One Day Internationals (ODIs), and Twenty20 (T20).
Test cricket is the longest format and is considered the ultimate test of a player's skill and endurance.

Cricket originated in England and is now widely played in countries like India, Australia,
England, Pakistan, South Africa, New Zealand, Sri Lanka, Bangladesh, and Nepal.

The game emphasizes teamwork, strategy, and sportsmanship.
